Lea's Homemade Alfredo

This is a tasty homemade alfredo that even aflredo haters will enjoy!

Ingredients:

    - any noodles you prefer

    - heavy whipping cream

    - minced garlic

    - parmesan cheese (preferdably frigo brand)

    - salt and pepper

    - onion powder

Instructions:

  1. First, start to boil your noodles. Heavily salt your water.

  2. While your noodles are boiling, start your sauce. Put butter and your minced garlic into your saucepan. Cook until fragrant or starting to brown.

  3. Once your garlic is how you want it, pour your heavy whipping cream in, stir until it starts to bubble.

  4. Once your cream is starting to boil, put your parmesan cheese in and stir slowly. Make sure your whipped cream and cheese get incorprated together.

  5. After your sauce is at a saucy consistency, you can throw in your spices to taste. That would be your salt, pepper, onion powder, and whatever else you like.

  6. Your sauce is done! Dont forget your noodles. When your noodles are how you like them, strain them and then throw them into the sauce.
*Alot of this recipe is a concept I like to call chaotic cooking. Be wary of how much you put in anything you cook, using this concept.*

Now enjoy your tasty alfredo!
